The digital world refers to the interconnected environment created by computers,
the internet, and digital technologies. It includes everything from websites, social media,
and online communication to artificial intelligence, cloud computing,
and virtual reality.

  • Key Aspects of the Digital World:

      • 1- !Internet & Connectivity – Websites, search engines, social media, and online services.
      2- !Communication – Emails, messaging apps, video calls, and social networks.
      3- !Digital Media – Streaming platforms, digital art, and online entertainment.
      4- !Artificial Intelligence & Automation – AI-powered applications, machine learning, and chatbots.
      5- !E-Commerce & Online Transactions – Online shopping, banking, and digital payments.
      6- !Cloud Computing – Storing and accessing data over the internet.
      7- ! Cybersecurity – Protecting data and systems from cyber threats.
      8- ! Metaverse & Virtual Reality – Digital spaces for interaction, gaming, and business.
